Privacy Policy

Privacy information for Lucente enquiries.

Last updated: 17 July 2026.

Information collected

The contact form may collect name, email address, enquiry type, message and privacy consent status. A hidden honeypot field is used to reduce spam.

Why information is collected

Submitted information is used to respond to enquiries about Lucente Corporate, its services, projects and possible collaborations.

How form submissions are delivered

The website includes a server-side Vercel function prepared to send enquiry emails through Resend when the required environment variables are configured. If it is not configured, visitors can use the direct email link to contact lucentecorporate@gmail.com.

Hosting logs and third-party services

The website is hosted on Vercel, which may process technical hosting logs needed to operate and protect the site. No analytics package is present in this repository.

Retention and control

Enquiries may be retained for a reasonable period to respond and keep project context. To request access or deletion, email lucentecorporate@gmail.com.

Important limits

Lucente Corporate does not sell submitted personal information. Users should not submit sensitive personal information through the form.
