About Lucente Corporate

A Sydney-based technology brand for practical digital products.

Lucente Corporate is a Sydney-based technology brand founded in 2025 by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman. It creates practical applications, digital products and flexible technology and design services. Lucente Corporate creates practical digital products and offers flexible technology and design services that turn ideas into useful, modern online experiences.

What Lucente Corporate is

Lucente Corporate is an independent technology brand founded in 2025 by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman. It is based in Sydney, Australia.

Purpose and approach

Lucente Corporate creates practical digital products and offers flexible technology and design services that turn ideas into useful, modern online experiences.

Lucente focuses on clear planning, careful interface design, accessible implementation and practical online experiences for individuals, teams and organisations.
