About Lucente Corporate

An independent technology and digital development brand.

Lucente Corporate is an independent technology and digital development brand founded by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman. Based in Sydney, Australia, Lucente creates websites, web applications, mobile application concepts, digital products and online brand experiences.

How Lucente began

Lucente began as a collection of independent technology projects connected by a shared interest in design, digital products and practical online tools.

What Lucente builds

The brand develops websites, web application concepts, mobile-first product ideas, digital identities and online experiences for modern audiences.

Design approach

Lucente’s design approach is minimal, structured and user-focused, with attention to typography, layout, accessibility and clear communication.

Technology and creativity

Lucente focuses on technology, usability and digital creativity, turning early ideas into planned, designed and developed digital experiences.
