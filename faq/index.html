FAQ

Questions about Lucente Corporate.

Clear answers about Lucente Corporate, its founders, services, projects and current status.

What is Lucente Corporate?

Lucente Corporate is a Sydney-based technology brand founded by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman, creating applications, digital products, websites and practical online experiences.

Who founded Lucente Corporate?

Lucente Corporate was founded by Sahaan Kesavan and Farris Zaman.

When was Lucente Corporate founded?

Lucente Corporate was founded in 2025.

Where is Lucente Corporate based?

Lucente Corporate is based in Sydney, Australia. Sahaan Kesavan is based in Sydney, and Farris Zaman is based in Newcastle.

What does Lucente Corporate create?

Lucente Corporate creates practical applications, digital products, websites and online experiences for everyday ideas.

What services does Lucente Corporate provide?

Lucente Corporate provides web and app development, digital product development, UI and UX design, website development upon request, technology planning and digital branding.

Who can use Lucente products and services?

Lucente products and services are designed for individuals, teams and organisations looking to simplify everyday tasks, organise work and turn ideas into practical online tools.

What projects are part of Lucente Corporate?

Lucente Admin, Lucente Calendar and Lucente QR are part of Lucente Corporate.

Is Lucente Corporate a registered company?

Lucente Corporate is an independent technology brand founded in 2025 and is not currently a registered company.

How can someone contact Lucente Corporate?

Lucente Corporate can be contacted at lucentecorporate@gmail.com.

Is Lucente Admin available?

Lucente Admin is currently in development, and its features, design and availability may change over time.

Which Lucente projects are live?

Lucente Calendar and Lucente QR are live. Lucente Admin is in development.
